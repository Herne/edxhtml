Steamed River Bass

Healthy and easy to make. Good for family feast - said me

List of Ingredients

  • River bass
  • Oyster source
  • Spring onion
  • Ginger
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Steam the fish for about 8 minutes
  2. Finely slice the sprint onion and ginger
  3. Heat the oil
  4. Place the sprint onion and ginger on top of the steamed fish
  5. Sprinkle oyster source on top
  6. Pour hot oil all over the fish, toppings, and source
  7. Enjoy
